The QAPI Centralized Database System

Soon after I was promoted to the Qaulity Management Department a need arrose for a centralized repository to collect and store quality metric data. This is when I proposed the idea for the development of QAPI Database. The database was originally created to house critical indicator data from 22 different departments from across the facility, but quickly grew into a complex information system used for everyday functionality within each individual department. The sky was the limit with endless possbilities for growth within the application.
